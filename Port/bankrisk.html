Implementing Predictive Analytics for Assessing a Bank's Credit Risk

Project Summary

  • Designed a predictive model to assess credit risk for banking clients as part of the Ethical Evidence-Based Decision Making course.
  • Applied data-driven techniques to evaluate borrower risk profiles and integrated argument maps to present ethical considerations and justifications.
  • Demonstrated how predictive analytics can support responsible, transparent, and ethically sound lending decisions.
