Explore Trends in Online Educations

Background & Topic

The project analyzes the Online Courses Usage and History Dataset to explore trends in online education.

The rise of online learning—especially post-pandemic—has made it important to understand user engagement, course popularity, and completion rates.

Source: Kaggle – Online Courses Dataset.

  • Demographics (age, region)
  • Course preferences (topics, duration)
  • Engagement (time spent)
  • Completion vs. dropout rates

Descriptive Statistics

  • Avg Duration: 55.14 hrs
  • Completion Rate: 75.12%
  • Avg Rating: 3.99 / 5
  • Price Range: ~$10 to ~$200

Insight: Premium pricing doesn't deter engagement if ratings are high.

No significant differences across categories. Focus on Data Science for its higher completion (48.7%).

Price and completion rates are nearly uncorrelated. Consider price as a commitment driver instead.

Three user segments exist. Final clusters converge on similar price & completion values. Targeted marketing recommended.

Factors Identified: Value (price/duration) & Quality (completion/rating).

Recommendations

  1. Set pricing thresholds based on category.
  2. Design with engagement in mind.
  3. Segment marketing by learner type.
  4. Focus on top-performing categories.
